Our Services

Comprehensive financial solutions designed to help your business succeed

OUTSOURCED ACCOUNTING

CSRInsights provides outsourced accounting services with a full team (CFO, Controller, Accounting Manager, and Staff Accountant) of accounting professionals to tackle all your finance and accounting needs.

FINANCE CONSULTING

Our outsourced financial planning and consulting team partners with your in-house accounting team, bookkeeper, or staff accountant(s) to create clarity and confidence in your company's financial reporting.

OPERATIONAL REPORTING

Weekly financial reports and monthly metrics offer clarity and the ability for a business owner to do strategic planning with certainty for their company's future business needs.

CASH FLOW FORECASTING

Cash flow planning allows a company to do strategic planning with confidence in the numbers. Accurate forecasting allows business owners to predict when cash shortfalls might occur and plan accordingly.

EXIT PLANNING | M&A

Companies looking to sell must have impeccable finances. Clean books, accurate forecasting, and a clear view of the business allows potential buyers to move through due diligence smoothly.

OUTSOURCED CFO SERVICES

Whether you're trying to optimize an exit or seeking investor funding, having a CFO on your team gives you the strategic financial leadership needed to drive growth and maximize company value.

BOOKKEEPING

Precise and current financial records are the bedrock of a sustainable business. Our professional bookkeeping services ensure accurate transaction management, reconciliation, and financial reporting.

ACCOUNTING SERVICES

Comprehensive accounting services including general ledger management, financial statement preparation, and compliance reporting to keep your business running smoothly.

AUDIT PREPARATION & SUPPORT

Professional audit preparation and support services to ensure your financial statements are accurate, compliant, and audit-ready. We help you navigate the audit process with confidence.
