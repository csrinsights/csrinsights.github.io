Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our accounting services

What accounting software do you work with?

We work with all major accounting platforms including QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, and Sage. Our team is trained on the latest versions and can seamlessly integrate with your existing systems or help you transition to a new platform.

How quickly can you start providing services?

We can typically begin services within 1-2 weeks of our initial consultation. The timeline depends on the complexity of your current setup and the scope of services needed. We prioritize a smooth transition with minimal disruption to your operations.

Are your services GAAP compliant?

Yes, all our accounting services are fully GAAP (Generally Accepted Accounting Principles) compliant. Our team stays current with all accounting standards and regulatory requirements to ensure your financial statements meet industry standards.

What size businesses do you work with?

We serve businesses of all sizes, from startups to established enterprises. Our scalable solutions are designed to grow with your business, whether you're processing dozens or thousands of transactions monthly.

How do you ensure data security?

Data security is our top priority. We use bank-level encryption, secure cloud storage, multi-factor authentication, and regular security audits. Our team follows strict confidentiality protocols and all data is backed up regularly.
