Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about outsourcing your accounting to CSRInsights

What is outsourced accounting and how does it work?

Outsourced accounting means partnering with an external provider like CSRInsights to handle your accounting functions. We manage your financial records, prepare reports, and provide insights while you maintain control over strategic decisions. Our team becomes an extension of your business, working with your existing systems and processes.

How much can I save by outsourcing accounting?

Most businesses save 25-40% compared to maintaining in-house accounting staff. You eliminate costs for salaries, benefits, training, software licenses, and infrastructure while gaining access to a team of experienced professionals and advanced technology platforms.

What accounting systems do you support?

We work with all major accounting platforms including QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, Sage, and other enterprise systems. Our team is trained across multiple platforms and can work within your existing system or help you implement a solution that better fits your needs.

How do you ensure the security of our financial data?

Data security is paramount. We utilize bank-level encryption, secure cloud infrastructure, multi-factor authentication, and strict access controls. Our team follows rigorous confidentiality protocols, and all data is regularly backed up. We comply with industry security standards and regulations to protect your sensitive financial information.

Can you handle industry-specific accounting requirements?

Yes, our team has extensive experience across multiple industries including healthcare, e-commerce, manufacturing, professional services, technology, non-profits, and government contractors. We understand industry-specific regulations, compliance requirements, and best practices to provide tailored accounting solutions.
