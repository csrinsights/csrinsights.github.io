Core Responsibilities

Comprehensive bookkeeping support to manage your daily financial operations

Transaction Management

Upload and process bank statements, credit card transactions, and other financial documents through secure platforms, ensuring all data is properly captured and organized.

Categorization & Classification

Accurately classify and code transactions according to your chart of accounts, maintaining consistency for reliable financial reporting and analysis.

Revenue Recognition

Match customer payments to corresponding invoices and sales records, ensuring accurate accounts receivable tracking and revenue documentation.

Account Reconciliation

Perform regular bank and credit card reconciliations to verify accuracy, identify discrepancies, and maintain the integrity of your financial records.

Record Maintenance

Organize and maintain comprehensive financial documentation, ensuring proper filing and easy retrieval for audits, reviews, and reporting needs.

Month-End Support

Assist with month-end closing procedures, ensuring all transactions are recorded, accounts are balanced, and financial data is ready for review.
